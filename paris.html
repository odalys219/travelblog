Paris, FR

Paris is the capital of France. It is located in Europe and is one of the world's largest cities. It is well-known as a center of art, fashion, food, culture, and history. With its long history, Paris has become one of the world's leading cities thanks to its extensive street lighting, earning the city the nickname "City of Light." It is also known as the City of Love due to the large number of romantic novels and films set in Paris, which inspired the name after the city's constant romanticism and popularity as a destination for couples.

With over 30 million tourists visiting this destination, the most common age group is those aged 25 to 44. This is an excellent destination for young and mature adults who want to experience culture beyond the beaches. This is also an excellent choice for those who prefer to focus on the country and what it has to offer rather than a tropical destination. This is a popular honeymoon destination, ideal for couples looking to explore a new country together after celebrating a significant milestone in their lives. This city does require you to know at least some basic French but many residents do speak English.

Some Places to Visit:

  • The Eiffel Tower
  • Louvre Museum
  • Cathédrale Notre-Dame de Paris
  • Musée d'Orsay
  • Arc de Triomphe
  • Disneyland Paris

Fun Facts:

  • Paris was originally a Roman City called "Lutetia".
  • There are at least three replicas of The Statu of Liberty in Paris.
  • Visitors who climb the Eiffel Tower have to walk 1,665 steps to reach the top.
  • The underground catacombs of Paris hold the remains of six million people.
  • Escargot is a traditional meal in Paris which are edible snails.
  • Paris has only one stop sign.
